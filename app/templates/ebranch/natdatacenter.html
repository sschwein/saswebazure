How Do We Measure the United States as a Nation?


A nation is the sum of its people, united under one governing body. Thus the success of a nation is directly reflected in the success of its people. Some major questions you may want to consider include:
1) Are the people safe from harm?
2) Are they provided a high level of health appropriate care in their environment?
3) Are they treated with equal fairness?
4) Do they have the freedoms and opportunities to do what they wish, with respect to others?
5) Can they seek education expand and improve their mind and skills?
6) Are they able to employ themselves in a fullfilling way?
