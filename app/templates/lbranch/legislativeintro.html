Welcome to the TGC Legislative tool!

Our founding fathers intended the legislative branch to be our voice in the fedreral government. This is the branch where the collective will of the people is represented outside of elections. It is where bills are written, sponsored, and enacted law. We all have three legislators who are there to generate laws and policies that reflect our interests. TGC is here to help you connect to, and judge whether or not your representatives are doing a good job, and whether or not you support the bills before our congress.
